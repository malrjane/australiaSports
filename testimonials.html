Sarah's Journey: From Couch to 42km

"I never thought of myself as a runner. The idea of running for the bus was daunting enough. But signing up for the local 5k fun run changed everything."

Three years later, Sarah crossed the finish line of the Gold Coast Marathon. It wasn't just about the physical training; it was the community support, the early morning group runs in Sydney, and the sheer determination to push past her limits.

"The Australian running community is unlike any other. They don't care how fast you are, just that you keep moving forward."
