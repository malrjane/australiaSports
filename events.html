Race Prep

Essential advice for Aussie conditions.

Hydration in Aussie Heat

Australian summers can reach temperatures above 35°C. Start hydrating 48 hours before your event. Carry more water than you think you need, and utilize every aid station on the course.

Course Elevation Maps

Never underestimate the terrain. Download GPX files for trail runs and study the elevation profile. Coastal runs often have deceptive headwinds that add resistance equivalent to climbing.

Registration Deadlines

Popular events like the Melbourne Marathon sell out months in advance. We recommend registering at least 12 weeks prior to race day to secure your spot and commit to your training block.
