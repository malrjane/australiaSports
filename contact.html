Frequently Asked Questions

How do I edit my event listing?

If you need to update dates or details for an existing listing, simply send us a message via the form above with the subject "Update Listing" and provide the link to your event.

Is listing an event free?

Yes! Basic community listings are 100% free. We believe in supporting the Australian sports culture. We also offer premium featured spots for larger races.

What are your advertising rates?

We have various packages for brands looking to reach active Australians. Select "Advertising" in the inquiry form to request our media kit.

Do you cover international events?

Currently, AUS:ACTIVE is focused exclusively on events within Australia and New Zealand. We want to be the best local resource possible.
